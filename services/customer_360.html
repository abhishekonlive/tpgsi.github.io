TurningPoint BI solutions provide decision makers with business intelligence (BI) they need to make faster, better informed decisions.

TurningPoint BI solutions – includes data scrubbing and consolidation , ad hoc reporting, stunning self-service data visualization, state of the art dashboards and predictive analytics – has helped businesses to improve performance and become better in everything they do. Our BI Solutions convert data into actionable Information via compelling, insightful dashboard reports and visualizations.

TurningPoint helps identify critical information within our customer’s organization ,cleans, authenticates, consolidates and maintains complete, accurate, and authoritative master data which further powers reliable business reporting and prudent decision making.

TurningPoint BI solutions have helped multiple businesses achieve operational excellence by providing transaction analysis and comparison reports to clearly identify areas of overlap or inefficiency.
