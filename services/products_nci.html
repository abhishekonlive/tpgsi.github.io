NiOS Carrier Interface

A modern solution to your interconnection needs supporting LSOG ordering and utilizing the latest technology.

 Let's Start a Conversation

NIOS Carrier Interface (NCI) streamlines and fully automates the complexities of telecom interconnection supporting the latest LSOG version while meeting all applicable FCC mandates. NCI implements a fully automated solution from number porting services to delivering directory options. NCI incorporates the entire complexity of acknowledgements, negotiations and timing making the task of customer acquisition and maintenance very simple for you.

NCI is a single automated interface that initiates, tracks and manages all your preorder and order requests to carriers. This includes complete pre-validation to reduce fallout and lower costs. Our solution is capable of accepting your orders in any format then augmenting and translating that transaction to meet the specifications of your carrier trading partner. Our system automatically handles this complexity so you don’t have to.

TRAM – Transaction Reporting and Management – is the work queue management and reporting tool that provides the tools to more effectively manage your order queues, process orders faster and get the deep insight you need to more effectively manage your business. Together NCI and TRAM provide very accurate and fast order management, fallout management, automated assignment and reporting. This solution provides complete visibility and transparency into your order processing at any stage.

NCI provides Support for all standard request types for wireline-to-wireline service ordering,

  • Automated ordering from initiation through activation
  • Ability to accept any format for orders and transform it to comply with trading partner requirements
  • Option to utilize powerful graphical user interface (GUI) or application programming interface (API) to access platforms
  • Robust order validation ensure less fallout and request rejection.
  • Secure hosted environment accessed via roles based secure login with multiple layers of access management
  • Provision for full reporting stack and comprehensive roles based management dashboard
  • Manage DL, E911, CNAM and LIDB updates
  • Fully compliant and trustworthy environment
