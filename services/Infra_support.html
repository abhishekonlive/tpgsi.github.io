Infrastructure Support Services

Derive optimal performance by ensuring high availability, high performance, high service levels from TurningPoint

 Let's Start a Conversation

At TurningPoint we ensure that your business applications and server infrastructure, both physical and in cloud, are managed and operated on a 24X7 basis to provide secure, high performance platform within your budget constraints. Our Application support expertise offers end to end support services for applications and middleware platforms and our infrastructure management skills provide 24 X 7 availability and zero down time.

Infrastructure Support

  • Manage production and pre-production environments for large scale B2B & B2C transaction systems includes :
    • Installation, configuration, monitoring & maintenance of server infrastructure.
    • Virtualization and cloud services.
    • OS & Database maintenance & support.
    • Storage management.
    • Migration and consolidation services
    • Disaster Recovery
  • 24x7 system monitoring
  • Network Design and Engineering
  • IT Security and DNS Infrastructure Protection

Application Support

Includes 24x7 monitoring of applications for availability / performance / capacity monitoring / management and incident management activities such as incident recording, initial investigation of issues, tracking and escalation management, bridge management, closure, user administration and service reporting.

Production support

Involves a detailed analysis of incident, incident resolution, and problem management activities such as trend analysis, root cause analysis, known error establishment and break-fixes.

Middleware support

Includes product administration activities such as monitoring, user administration, application deployment, patch management, and configuration management for application infrastructure like web servers, application servers and all middleware which supports queueing, BI, ESB, caching, Hadoop clusters, load balancers and other type of application feature enablers.

Release and change support

Comprises setting up of various environments, release / change activities such as release coordination, emergency releases, build and code deployment and configuration management.

Creation of Known Issue database

Creating, recording and updating knowledge base for known issues and their resolutions, updating SOPS, for both knowledge reuse and sharing.
