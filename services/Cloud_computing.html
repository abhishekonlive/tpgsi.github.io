At TurningPoint we develop and then deploy and manage your solutions on cloud. Deploying on cloud harnesses the power of servers, as well as virtualization technologies. Four different deployment models for implementing cloud technology are available - private, public, hybrid, community, and each has its advantages and disadvantages with significant implications.

To have the edge amongst competitors, selection of the right type of cloud hosting is needed. Choosing the right hosting means customers can achieve business goals more easily. Efforts can be channelized to achieve strategic steps to help businesses succeed. We at Turning point have in depth knowledge about each of the 4 deployment models and help our customers choose the right model for their deployments based on their business models. We therefore help maximize the advantages that businesses achieve from cloud.

Few benefits of moving to the Cloud :

Providing scalability

Moving to clouds provides operational agility for businesses with growing or fluctuating bandwidth demands due to cloud’s ability to scale capacity up or down as per need.

Capital Expenditure free

Moving to the clouds means not investing in the high cost of hardware. Moreover it combines ease of setup and management with pay as you go or subscription model for the services.

Competitiveness

Moving to the cloud gives access to enterprise-class technology at affordable rates while remaining lean and nimble, helping organizations to compete at par with any already established competitors.

Easy maintenance

Cloud service providers take care of all software updates, security updates or any other maintenance activities for you. Customers can therefore focus on the things that matter, like growing their business.
