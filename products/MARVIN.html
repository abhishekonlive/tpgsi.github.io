Marvin™ is an identity resolution BI tool, that enables companies and government organizations to search for and match name identity data. Name identity data matching or Identity resolution is an operational intelligence process, typically powered by an identity resolution engine or middleware, whereby organizations can connect disparate data sources with a view to understanding possible identity matches Marvin™ helps organizations to connect disparate data sources with a view to understanding possible identity matches across multiple data silos. Manage risk, detect fraud, and ensure compliance with regulations by using Marvin™.

Accurate Matching

Marvin™ provides highly accurate matching of entity records increasing your operational efficiency and effectiveness by detecting any sort of fraud or money laundering or non compliance against regulations

Scalable

Highly scalable identity resolution for Big Data environments. It is a useful tool for use within Customer Data Integration (CDI) and Master Data Management (MDM) systems also.

High performance

Performs identity resolution against tens of millions or more entity records with sub-second response times.

Highly Flexible

The embedded robust parsing component, as well as the flexible and thorough matching technology, dramatically reduces the need for preliminary data cleansing.

Ease of deployment and use

Marvin™ provides a very comprehensive easy to deploy and use interface. It helps manage risk, detect fraud, and ensure compliance with regulations, thereby enhancing security by detecting entity records of interest with high accuracy
