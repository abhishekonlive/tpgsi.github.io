Transaction Reporting and Management (TRAM )

Intelligent, Intuitive, Impressive

 Let's Start a Conversation

TRAM, a part of NIOS® product line, is a completely automated and integrated ticketing solution for Work assignment, productivity tracking and reporting with no manual intervention or effort. TRAM fast-tracks your ticketing process by eliminating manual bottlenecks and errors and establishing a smooth flow from start to fulfillment of work.

Intelligent

Intelligent orchestration from start to successful fulfillment, helps you stay connected with the ticket you're watching or you have created or been assigned.

Intuitive

Easy to understand, access and comprehensive reporting with multiple slice and dice options will help provide valuable insights into your business immediately.

Impressive

State of the art look and feel with ease of use and easy navigation will definitely impress you and reduce training costs.

Transparency

TRAM® provides full visibility into your end-to-end work management. Also you may search, display, export, update, and reference all data within a single interface.

Automation

TRAM® is a completely automated solution with no manual intervention from assignment to fulfillment thereby reducing time for start to work delivery.

Flexible and configurable

It incorporates rules and exceptions management, which can be used to customize the solution as per your business needs

Role based access control

Complete access control with role based accesses for multiple roles as per organizational needs is possible within TRAM®.

Comprehensive

Team and resource information management are integrated into the same solution, thus providing a single platform to work on.

Intelligent Orchestration

Workflow with no manual intervention and configurable routing strategies provides you maximum flexibility to implement your organizations business goals.

Elaborate Dashboard

Elaborate configurable dashboard that helps increase your team’s productivity with lightning fast insights and instant data visualizations. The easy to use reports, makes it very convenient for users to prepare, analyze and visualize complex data and draw conclusions from it. An innovative, stunningly beautiful and comprehensive management dashboard provides a kaleidoscopic view of the data.

Productivity Management

Configurable ,prioritized queue management with concurrency control ensures that the work gets done at the right time and in the right way. The solution also provides comparative reporting amongst individuals or groups for performance management and tuning.

Cost effective

A feature rich solution at a very reasonable price, that does not burn a hole in your pocket. Also TRAM® helps you decreases your cost by reducing staffing needs and operational costs.
