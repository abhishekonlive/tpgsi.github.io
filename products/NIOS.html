NIOS

Making the complex simple for you

Gain enterprise-wide visibility to proactively manage and optimize network and telecom assets using TurningPoint developed - Network Inventory and Optimization Solution (NiOS®), a comprehensive software solution. NiOS® helps to eliminate unneeded telecom expenses, simplify continuity of operations (COOP) planning, and facilitate compliance with OMB Circular A-123.

NiOS® supports the full communications lifecycle from procurement to payment.

NIOS Carrier Interface (NCI®), a gateway powered by the NIOS® engine, extends to the carrier and helps in telecom ordering and provisioning services

Manage

Manage telecom move, add, change, and disconnect (MACD) activity by managing provisioning requests through highly adaptive NiOS®

Optimize

Optimize wireless rate plans and wired network services. Identify and remove unneeded wireless devices and wired services

Complete contract management

Maintain full, detailed and configurable contract information and use it for validation and expense forecast.

Reduce Expenses

Save over 25% on total expenditure. Recover overpayments due to erroneous bills. Reconcile and verify telecom bills for wired, wireless, and network & telecommunications equipment assets.

Automation

Reduce headcount by automating telecom management. Implement a completely automated process of invoice receipt, allocation, validation, approval, and payment for fixed telecom billing with NiOS®.

Reporting

Intuitive reports with field selection and filters that provide compact data for orders flow, real time inventory status, current billing, forecasted expenses as well as exceptions, at all levels - agency or complete organization.

Provision

A highly flexible solution that helps provision all services across all contracts and all suppliers

Best fit Selection

Identify the most favorable contract to use for each service order

Dashboard

Role based dashboards for both users , approval authorities and management. Management dashboard provides full visibility of asset usage, tracking and reporting of asset usage against forecast thus providing valuable operational information to the management and enabling optimal use of assets.

Use NiOS® to power your enterprise with a centralized and accurate inventory of all of your telecom services, vendors and expenses. Through NiOS® gain an understanding of your assets and spending and experience sizeable savings based on the usage of the services.
