About Us

Vision

We offer cost effective solutions with high quality services thereby creating value for our customers. We provide tailor-made, highly cost effective solutions and tangible benefits without comprising on performance

TPSS was established in 2002 by two technologists who have had worked in the field of Telecommunication Systems over several decades. Over the past 13 years TPSS has charted an impressive track record in customer satisfaction, innovation, sales and profitability. TPSS is privately funded and completely debt free.

The success of the company has been made possible with the continuous enhancement of competence in technology, project management and customer focus. Our heritage of innovation and strong service capabilities enables us to provide our full suite of mission-critical solutions in a timely and cost effective manner. The solutions provided by TPSS are designed, developed and integrated by passionate and well qualified engineers whose collective experience in the IT industry spans several decades.

TPSS believes in long-term relationship with its customers and nurtures the customer relationship through commitment, trust and confidence. We take pride in our longstanding reputation of predictability, dependability and corporate decision-making that delivers meaningful results and preserves the longevity of our client partnerships. Our happy repeat customer base exemplifies the high level of customer satisfaction with our solutions.

TPSS believes that each employee should be adequately trained to perform his or her roles effectively and efficiently. Wide spectrum of training is planned throughout the year in the areas of software skills, hardware skills, quality, soft skills, personality development and management.

Leadership

Bangalore S. Shivacharan

Chief Executive Officer

Bangalore is responsible for strategic growth and managing overall operations of TurningPoint with a focus on our customer delivery organization and associated technology and services. Bangalore has over 25 years of Engineering and Management experience encompassing both commercial and public sectors. Prior to starting TurningPoint, Bangalore was the co-founder and Chief Technology Officer of Wisor Telecom Corporation where he designed and built next generation Telecommunications Operational Support Systems for the competitive local exchange carrier and enterprise market. Bangalore has extensive experience in distributed object oriented, high transaction enterprise system architecture, information security framework, and Internet and wireless communication technologies.

Satya Govindu

Assistant Vice President of Software Engineering

Satya is responsible for managing commercial delivery teams, and responsible for managing research & development teams at TurningPoint Inda. During his 20 year career, he has worked in research lab environments in both technical and management roles. Prior to joining TurningPoint in 2010, Satya worked for Bell Northern Research, Nortel Networks, AT&T Labs, Lucent Labs, IBM Global Services, and Tyco Submarine Systems. Satya has extensive knowledge of software architecture, standards-based large scale software systems, and is adept at leading and motivating highly technical teams.

Satya holds a Master of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Texas at Arlington, USA.

M.R. Vishvesh

Sr Manager - Finance and Admin

Vishvesh as he is known within the organization , is the Senior Manager responsible for both Finance and Administration. His responsibilities include corporate finance, financial planning and record-keeping, as well as financial reporting to higher management. He is responsible for the management ,administration and security of the company's facilities.

He has been with TurningPoint for more than 10 years now. He lives in Bangalore with his wife and 2 kids.

Veena Gurusiddaiah
