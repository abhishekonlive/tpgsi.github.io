About Us

Integrate seamless, secure and reliable information flow and transaction processing into systems development

Who we are…

At TurningPoint Global Solutions, our heritage of innovation and strong service capabilities enables us to provide our full suite of mission-critical solutions in a timely and cost effective manner. The high level of customer satisfaction with our solutions is exemplified by our happy repeat customer base. We take pride in our longstanding reputation of predictability, dependability and corporate decision-making that delivers meaningful results and preserves the longevity of our client partnerships.  Founded in 2002, we are privately funded and debt free.

What we do…

We specialize in telecom lifecycle management, Health IT, software development & integration, IT governance consulting, information assurance & cybersecurity, systems infrastructure support, and business process improvement.

Government Sector

TurningPoint has earned a reputation for reliable performance among our customers, including VA, FEMA, CMS, FDA, CPSC, DoD, NTSB, DHS ICE and DOT. We assist federal agencies to expedite technology upgrades and/or contract transitions, proactively manage telecom and network assets, implement enterprise level governance, and business process improvement, all while routinely delivering cost savings. Our Federal telecom customers have saved 9-23% on their total annual telecom expenditures.

Private Sector

TurningPoint assists our commercial clients, who include Neustar, FreddieMac, Qwest, Accenture, Time Warner Cable, Level 3, Verizon Business, and others, to reduce risk, reduce cost, accelerate delivery time, and gain a competitive advantage. TurningPoint’s proactive commercial solutions are upheld by stringent Service Level Agreements (SLAs) to ensure service delivery is aligned with the priorities of our diversified clientele. 30,000 users (and counting) from 330 of the Fortune 500 Companies currently depend on systems that we support.

Our Methodology…

Industry best practices

We apply industry best practices and standards, such as ITIL, ISO, CMMI, EVM, and PMBOK to achieve superior results.ITIL is the most widely accepted approach to IT service management in the world. ITIL can help individuals and organizations use IT.

CMMI Maturity Level

Turning Point has been independently assessed at CMMI Maturity Level 3. This means that we have successfully demonstrated a standardized approach to 18 distinct process areas, and apply these processes across all development projects.
